## Who to Interview

**Best candidates for customer interview:**
- Any adult child or spouse of someone in a nursing home, rehab facility, or memory care unit
- A nurse, CNA, or social worker at any SNF or long-term care facility
- A hospital discharge planner who sends patients to SNFs

**Finding them:**
- Ask family friends, ward members, neighbors
- Post in r/AgingParents or Facebook caregiver groups
- Ask your bishop — someone in your ward almost certainly has a family member in a care facility

## Two Problems to Score

1. **Family Disconnection** — Adult family members who feel in the dark about daily SNF care
2. **Nurse Interruption** — SNF nursing staff interrupted by repetitive family phone calls

Both point to the same product (CareBridge Connect) from two sides of the same pain.
